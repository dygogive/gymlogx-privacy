Privacy Policy for GymLogX

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Overview

GymLogX is a workout tracking and training planning application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Data Collection

GymLogX currently does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The app operates entirely offline.

Data Storage

All data you create in GymLogX is stored locally on your device:

  • Workout programs and training blocks
  • Exercises and their configurations
  • Workout results (weight, reps, sets, time)
  • Performance history and progress data

This data is stored using Android Room Database on your device and remains under your complete control.

Data Backup and Export

You have full control over your data:

  • You can manually export your entire training database to a JSON file
  • Export files are stored on your device in a location you choose
  • You can import previously exported data to restore your workout history

Permissions

GymLogX may require the following permissions:

  • Storage/Files: To export your workout data to JSON files and import backup files from your device storage

Pre-loaded Content

GymLogX includes two sample workout programs for demonstration purposes:

  • Gym workout (standard equipment)
  • Outdoor workout (minimal equipment)

Disclaimer: These sample programs are provided as examples to help you understand how to use the app. They are not personalized training plans. Before starting any exercise program, consult with a qualified healthcare provider or fitness professional. The developer assumes no responsibility for any injuries or health issues that may result from using these sample programs or any workout programs created within the app.

Third-Party Services

GymLogX currently does not use any third-party services.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Photos or media files
  • Contact information
  • Financial information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

GymLogX does not include in-app purchases or subscription features.

Age Restrictions

GymLogX is intended for users aged 16 and above. Since the app stores all data locally on the device and does not connect to the internet, no personal information is collected from any users.

Data Security

Your workout data is stored securely in an encrypted database on your device. We recommend:

  • Regularly exporting your data as a backup
  • Using your device's security features (screen lock, encryption)
  • Keeping backup files in a secure location

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device.

User Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Export: You can export all your data to a JSON file
  • Delete: You can delete individual workouts, exercises, or entire programs within the app
  • Complete removal: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device

When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and notify you through the app if the changes are significant. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
