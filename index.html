Privacy Policy for GymLogX

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Overview

GymLogX is a workout tracking and training planning application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Key Point: Your workout data stays on your device. We only collect anonymous technical data to improve app stability.

Data Collection

GymLogX collects minimal data to improve app stability and user experience.

Local Data (Stored on Your Device)

All your personal workout data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers:

  • Workout programs and training blocks
  • Exercises and their configurations
  • Workout results (weight, reps, sets, time)
  • Performance history and progress data

Automatically Collected Technical Data (via Firebase)

To improve app stability and performance, we collect the following technical, non-personal data through Google Firebase:

Firebase Crashlytics

  • Crash reports when the app unexpectedly closes
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Stack traces (information about what caused the crash)
  • Anonymous device identifiers

Firebase Analytics

  • Basic app usage statistics (e.g., which screens are viewed, session duration)
  • Device type and operating system
  • Country/region (based on IP address, not precise location)

Purpose: This data is used to identify and fix bugs, understand feature usage, and improve app performance. No workout or personal information is collected.

Data Storage

  • Workout data remains on your device only
  • Firebase technical data is transmitted securely to Google and stored according to their policies
  • No personally identifiable data (name, email, location) is collected

Data Backup and Export

You have full control over your workout data:

  • You can manually export your training database to a JSON file
  • Export files are stored on your device in a location you choose
  • You can import previously exported data to restore your workout history

Permissions

  • Storage/Files: To export your workout data and import backups
  • Internet: Required for Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics to send anonymous technical data

Third-Party Services

Google Firebase

We use Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous technical data:

  • Data collected: Crash reports, device information, and usage statistics
  • Purpose: Improve app stability and performance
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Firebase data is managed and stored by Google according to their privacy policies. We do not share data with any third parties other than Google Firebase.

How We Use Collected Data

  • Identify and fix app crashes
  • Understand feature usage patterns
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Make informed decisions for future features

Opting Out

You can disable Firebase data collection by blocking internet access for the app in your device settings. All core functionality remains available offline.

Pre-loaded Content

The app includes two sample workout programs to help you get started:

  • Gym workout (standard equipment)
  • Outdoor workout (minimal equipment)

Age Restrictions

GymLogX is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Data Security

  • Workout data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security measures
  • Firebase data is transmitted securely over HTTPS
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect any data transmitted through Firebase

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and export your workout data at any time through the app's export feature
  • Delete your workout data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Opt out of Firebase data collection by disabling internet access for the app
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns or questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised
  • Significant changes will be announced in the app's "What's New" section on Google Play Store
  • Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy

Legal Compliance

GymLogX complies with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • Google Firebase Terms of Service
  • Applicable data protection regulations

Medical Disclaimer

GymLogX is a workout tracking tool, not a medical device or personalized training service. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before starting any exercise program, especially if you have any pre-existing health conditions.
