Privacy Policy for GymLogX

Last updated: January 19, 2026

What's New (January 19, 2026): We have disabled Advertising Identifier (Ad ID) collection. This policy now reflects that NO Ad ID is collected by GymLogX.

Overview

GymLogX is a workout tracking and training planning application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Key Point: Your workout data stays 100% on your device. We only collect anonymous technical data to improve app stability. We do NOT collect Advertising ID (Ad ID).

Data Collection

GymLogX collects minimal data solely to improve app stability and performance.

Local Data (Stored on Your Device)

All personal workout data is stored locally and not transmitted externally:

  • Workout programs and training blocks
  • Exercises and their configurations
  • Workout results (weight, reps, sets, time)
  • Performance history and progress data

Automatically Collected Technical Data (via Firebase)

We use Google Firebase to collect anonymous technical data:

Firebase Crashlytics

  • Crash reports when the app unexpectedly closes
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Stack traces (information about what caused the crash)
  • Anonymous device identifiers (randomly generated UUID, not linked to your identity)

Firebase Analytics

  • Basic app usage statistics (e.g., which screens are viewed, session duration)
  • Device type and operating system
  • Country/region based on IP address (no precise location)
  • Anonymous session data
IMPORTANT - What We DO NOT Collect:
  • ❌ Advertising Identifier (Ad ID) - We have explicitly disabled Ad ID collection
  • ❌ Your name, email, or phone number
  • ❌ Precise or approximate location (GPS)
  • ❌ Photos, files, or media
  • ❌ Any personally identifiable information
  • ❌ Your workout data (stored locally only)

Purpose: This data is used to identify and fix bugs, understand feature usage, and improve app performance. No workout or personal information is collected. Anonymous device identifiers are used solely for technical improvement.

Data Storage

  • Workout data remains on your device only
  • Firebase technical data is transmitted securely to Google and stored according to their policies
  • No personally identifiable data (name, email, precise location) is collected

Data Backup and Export

You have full control over your workout data:

  • You can export your training database to a JSON file
  • Export files are stored on your device in a location you choose
  • You can import previously exported data to restore your workout history

Permissions

  • Storage/Files: To export and import workout backups
  • Internet: Required for Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics to send anonymous technical data

Third-Party Services

Google Firebase

We use Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous technical data:

  • Data collected: Crash reports, device information, anonymous usage statistics
  • NOT collected: Advertising Identifier (Ad ID) - explicitly disabled
  • Purpose: Improve app stability and performance
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

By using GymLogX, you consent to the collection of anonymous technical data as described herein. No personal or workout data leaves your device.

How We Use Collected Data

  • Identify and fix app crashes
  • Understand feature usage patterns
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Make informed decisions for future features

Opting Out

You have multiple options to control data collection:

  • Disable Firebase data collection by blocking internet access for the app
  • All core functionality remains available offline
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data

Pre-loaded Content

The app includes two sample workout programs to help you get started:

  • Gym workout (standard equipment)
  • Outdoor workout (minimal equipment)

Data Security

  • Workout data stays on your device and relies on your device's security measures
  • Firebase data is transmitted securely over HTTPS
  • Industry-standard security practices are used for any data transmitted through Firebase

User Rights

  • Access and export workout data at any time via the app's export feature
  • Delete workout data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Opt out of Firebase data collection by disabling internet access
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns or questions
