Privacy Policy for GymLogX

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Overview

GymLogX is a workout tracking and training planning application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Data Collection

GymLogX collects minimal data to improve app stability and user experience:

Local Data (Stored on Your Device)

All your personal workout data is stored locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to external servers:

  • Workout programs and training blocks
  • Exercises and their configurations
  • Workout results (weight, reps, sets, time)
  • Performance history and progress data

This data is stored using Android Room Database on your device and remains under your complete control.

Automatically Collected Data (via Firebase)

To improve app stability and performance, we collect the following anonymous technical data through Google Firebase services:

Firebase Crashlytics

  • Crash reports (when the app unexpectedly closes)
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Stack traces (technical information about what code caused the crash)
  • Device language settings

Purpose: This data helps us identify and fix bugs to improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Firebase Analytics

  • Basic app usage statistics (e.g., which screens are viewed)
  • App session duration
  • Device type and operating system
  • Country/region (based on IP address, not precise location)

Purpose: This data helps us understand how users interact with the app and improve the user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Important: Firebase data is aggregated and anonymized. We cannot identify individual users from this data. Your workout data (exercises, results, programs) is NEVER sent to Firebase or any external servers.

Data Storage

Local Storage (Your Device)

  • All workout data remains on your device
  • Stored using encrypted Android Room Database
  • Never transmitted to external servers

Firebase Storage (Google Servers)

  • Anonymous crash reports and analytics data are stored on Google's secure servers
  • Data is retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Stored in accordance with Google's security standards

Data Backup and Export

You have full control over your workout data:

  • You can manually export your entire training database to a JSON file
  • Export files are stored on your device in a location you choose
  • You can import previously exported data to restore your workout history

Permissions

GymLogX may require the following permissions:

  • Storage/Files: To export your workout data to JSON files and import backup files from your device storage
  • Internet: Required for Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics to send anonymous technical data

Third-Party Services

GymLogX uses the following third-party services:

Google Firebase

We use Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics (provided by Google LLC) to collect anonymous crash reports and usage statistics.

  • Data collected: Crash reports, device information, app usage statistics (as detailed above)
  • Purpose: App stability and performance improvement
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Data retention: 90 days

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers
  • Precise location data (only country/region for analytics)
  • Your workout data (exercises, results, programs) - this stays on your device
  • Photos or media files
  • Contact information
  • Financial information

How We Use Collected Data

Anonymous technical data collected via Firebase is used exclusively to:

  • Identify and fix app crashes and bugs
  • Understand which features are used most frequently
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Make informed decisions about future feature development

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use data for advertising purposes
  • Share data with partners for marketing
  • Track individual users

Opting Out

Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics are enabled by default to help us improve the app. If you wish to opt out:

  • You can disable crash reporting in the app settings (if available)
  • Alternatively, you can block the app's internet access through your device settings (note: this will prevent crash reports but won't affect core app functionality as it works offline)

Age Restrictions

GymLogX is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will delete it immediately.

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Your workout data is stored in an encrypted database on your device
  • Firebase data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
  • Google Firebase employs industry-standard security measures

We recommend:

  • Regularly exporting your data as a backup
  • Using your device's security features (screen lock, encryption)
  • Keeping backup files in a secure location

Data Retention

  • Local workout data: Remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Firebase crash reports and analytics: Automatically deleted after 90 days

Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored workout data from your device.

User Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your workout data within the app at any time
  • Export: You can export all your workout data to a JSON file
  • Delete: You can delete individual workouts, exercises, or entire programs within the app
  • Opt-out: You can disable internet access to prevent Firebase data collection
  • Complete removal: Uninstalling the app removes all local data; Firebase data expires after 90 days

International Data Transfers

Firebase data may be processed and stored on servers located in different countries, including the United States and European Union. Google Firebase complies with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR.
