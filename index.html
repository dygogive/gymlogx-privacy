Privacy Policy for GymLogX

Last updated: January 11, 2026

What's New: This update provides detailed information about our use of the Advertising Identifier (Ad ID) exclusively for analytics purposes. We've added clear explanations and user control instructions.

Overview

GymLogX is a workout tracking and training planning application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Key Point: Your workout data stays 100% on your device. We only collect anonymous technical data to improve app stability.

Advertising Identifier (Ad ID) - Important Clarification

NEW SECTION GymLogX uses Google Firebase services which may collect the Advertising Identifier (Ad ID) from your device exclusively for analytics purposes:

Why we need Ad ID:

  • Analytics attribution: To understand which app installations come from which sources
  • Crash reporting: To group crash reports by installation source
  • Usage statistics: To measure user engagement anonymously

Important details about Ad ID usage:

  • Ad ID is collected in anonymous, non-personalized form only
  • It is NOT linked to your identity, workout data, or personal information
  • We do not use Ad ID for advertising or share it with advertisers
  • It is used solely for technical improvement of the app

Your control over Ad ID:

You can control or reset your Advertising Identifier at any time:

  • Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • You may also opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Resetting creates a new anonymous identifier

Data Collection

GymLogX collects minimal data solely to improve app stability and performance.

Local Data (Stored on Your Device)

All personal workout data is stored locally and not transmitted externally:

  • Workout programs and training blocks
  • Exercises and their configurations
  • Workout results (weight, reps, sets, time)
  • Performance history and progress data

Automatically Collected Technical Data (via Firebase)

UPDATED We use Google Firebase to collect anonymous technical data:

Firebase Crashlytics

  • Crash reports when the app unexpectedly closes
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Stack traces (information about what caused the crash)
  • Anonymous device identifiers used for technical purposes

Firebase Analytics

  • UPDATED Basic app usage statistics (e.g., which screens are viewed, session duration)
  • Device type and operating system
  • Country/region based on IP address (no precise location)
  • NEW: Advertising Identifier (Ad ID) - collected anonymously for attribution as described above

Purpose: This data is used to identify and fix bugs, understand feature usage, and improve app performance. No workout or personal information is collected. Anonymous device identifiers are used solely for technical improvement.

Data Storage

  • Workout data remains on your device only
  • Firebase technical data is transmitted securely to Google and stored according to their policies
  • No personally identifiable data (name, email, precise location) is collected

Data Backup and Export

You have full control over your workout data:

  • You can export your training database to a JSON file
  • Export files are stored on your device in a location you choose
  • You can import previously exported data to restore your workout history

Permissions

  • Storage/Files: To export and import workout backups
  • Internet: Required for Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics to send anonymous technical data

  • Purpose: Improve app stability and performance

By using GymLogX, you consent to the collection of anonymous technical data as described herein. No personal or workout data leaves your device.
