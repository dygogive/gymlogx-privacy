Privacy Policy for GymLogX

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Overview

GymLogX is a workout tracking and training planning application that operates entirely offline. We are committed to protecting your privacy by not collecting, storing, or transmitting any of your personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

GymLogX does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The app does not connect to the internet and does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Data Storage

All data you create in GymLogX is stored locally on your device:

  • Workout programs and training blocks
  • Exercises and their configurations
  • Workout results (weight, reps, sets, time)
  • Performance history and progress data

This data is stored using Android Room Database and never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Data Backup and Export

You have full control over your data:

  • You can manually export your entire training database to a JSON file
  • Export files are stored on your device in a location you choose
  • You can import previously exported data to restore your workout history
  • Your data remains under your complete control at all times

Permissions

GymLogX may require the following permissions:

  • Storage/Files: To export your workout data to JSON files and import backup files from your device storage

These permissions are used only for the purposes described and your data never leaves your device without your explicit action.

Pre-loaded Content

GymLogX includes two sample workout programs for demonstration purposes:

  • Gym workout (standard equipment)
  • Outdoor workout (minimal equipment)

Disclaimer: These sample programs are provided as examples to help you understand how to use the app. They are not personalized training plans. Before starting any exercise program, consult with a qualified healthcare provider or fitness professional. The developer assumes no responsibility for any injuries or health issues that may result from using these sample programs or any workout programs created within the app.

Third-Party Services

GymLogX does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • No crash reporting services (e.g., Crashlytics)
  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking or monitoring services
  • No cloud storage or backup services

The app operates completely offline and your data stays on your device.

Internet Connectivity

GymLogX does not connect to the internet. The app functions entirely offline and does not transmit any data over the network.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The current version of GymLogX does not include in-app purchases or subscription features. If such features are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be notified of any changes.

Children's Privacy

GymLogX is intended for users aged 13 and above. The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the device and the app does not connect to the internet, no personal information is collected from any users, including minors.

Data Security

Your workout data is stored securely in a local database on your device. Since the app does not connect to the internet, your data is protected from online threats. We recommend:

  • Regularly exporting your data as a backup
  • Using your device's security features (screen lock, encryption)
  • Keeping backup files in a secure location

User Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Export: You can export all your data to a JSON file
  • Delete: You can delete individual workouts, exercises, or entire programs within the app
  • Complete removal: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

The developer is not responsible for any injuries, health issues, or other consequences resulting from the use of this app.
